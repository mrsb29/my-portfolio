Web Design

Project Info:

Developed a user-friendly and stress-free job portal platform for HireTop Skills, enabling job seekers to find their dream careers and employers to discover the right talent. The platform is designed to match skills and interests with relevant job openings while offering valuable resources to support users in their job search.

Project Details:
  • Project Name: HireTop Skills- (Job Portal)
  • URL:www.hiretopskills.com
Key Features:

    Job Listings, Company Directory, Candidate Profiles, Trending Job Categories, Featured Jobs,Application Process,Recent Articles,User Reviews.

Technologies Used:
  • Back-End:

    Laravel for the web framework, ensuring fast, scalable, and secure development.

  • Front-End:

    HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, and JavaScript to ensure a responsive and clean user interface.

  • Databases:

    MySQL for efficient storage and management of product and user data.

  • Hosting & Deployment:

    Hosted on AWS EC2 with Cloudflare for DNS management and security.

  • Integrations:

    Payment Gateway for online transactions and third-party APIs for document validation and verification.
