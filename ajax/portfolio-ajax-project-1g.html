Web Design

Project Info:

I developed a detailed and informative web platform for a Nutraceutical Product brand that focuses on integrative veterinary medicine. The platform highlights the use of conventional therapies and nutraceutical products, aimed at improving the health, behavior, performance, and food quality of patients. It also provides an in-depth look at the brand's history, philosophy, and commitment to enhancing wellness in animals through innovative treatments.

Project Details:
  • Project Name:Nutraceutical Product & History - (Veterinary Medicine Platform)
  • URL:www.adimanutra.com
Key Features:

    Product Information, Educational Content, Responsive Design,Product Availability & Updates, Product Ordering,e-commerce

Technologies Used:
  • Back-End:

    Laravel for the web framework, ensuring fast, scalable, and secure development.

  • Front-End:

    HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, and JavaScript to ensure a responsive and clean user interface.

  • Databases:

    MySQL for efficient storage and management of product and user data.

  • Hosting & Deployment:

    Hosted on AWS EC2 with Cloudflare for DNS management and security.

  • Integrations:

    Payment Gateway for online transactions and third-party APIs for document validation and verification.
